Undertaking Declaration

ADA INTERNSHIP — ACCEPTANCE OF TERMS & CONDITIONS

(a) I will abide by the working hours from 09 AM to 3.30PM during project work period. I am aware that I should have minimum 85% of attendance for subsequent award of certificate. I am aware that arrangement for accommodation, logistics have to be done by myself.

(b) I am aware that I am not eligible for any stipend. ADA will not be obliged to offer employment on completion of project work.

(c) I will maintain non-objectionable conduct during the entire period and will not mention or misrepresent anything regarding ADA on social media.

(d) I will not have access to internet or any LAN in ADA. I will not bring/use mobile phones, Laptops, USB dongles, or any storage media inside ADA.

(e) I shall keep and maintain strict confidentiality of all information, knowledge and data that may come to my possession and shall not disclose or divulge any such information to third parties.

(f) I will not reproduce, store, or transmit in any form any copyrighted material or confidential/proprietary material which is the property of ADA or its clients.

(g) Any demonstration of equipment or software developed during training will be carried out at ADA premises only, with permission from Technology Director.

(h) Publications, if any, will be cleared as per ADA procedures.

(i) Any violation to the laid down instructions will lead to immediate termination of my/group project work.

(j) I will indicate ADA scientist as co-guide in the project report.

(k) A copy of training/project report will be submitted to respective Technology Director, ADA.

(l) On completion I will immediately return/surrender all data, files, documents, and knowledge databases entrusted during project work.

(m) I will complete project work by due date and will not seek extension. On completion I will obtain clearance from guide and submit to Admin for certificate.

(n) I am aware that certificate will be issued per standard template and no re-issue of duplicate certificate will be made. I will not approach ADA officials for any recommendations in future.
